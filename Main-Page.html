About Us


Welcome to CIT Cinemas!

We are a the No.1 cinema theatre that provides an unmatched movie experience to our patrons. Established in 2007, CIT Cinemas has been a household name for movie enthusiasts in our community. Our theatre is located in the heart of the city, easily accessible to everyone. At CIT Cinemas, we believe that watching a movie is not just about the film, but the entire experience.

We have state-of-the-art projection systems that provide crystal-clear images and immersive sound that transports you into the world of the movies. We have multiple screens to cater to the diverse preferences of our patrons, ranging from the latest blockbuster movies to art-house films.
